Work + Fun

DD Dispatcher   A group project for our senior software engineering capstone class inspired by our collective frustration of parties' DD system. We want to create a system that allows group leaders, riders, and drivers an application that eases the pain of transportation to parties. Our application will allow group leaders to manage their users, DD's for an event or week, and send invitations to other users. Riders can request a DD similar to how a rider makes a request for an Uber. DD's for an event have all of their rides automated by the application as well as having a Google Maps widget automate the directions from ride to ride. We hope to remove the burdens of current DD systems (pen and paper -- yuck!!) and prevent drunk driving. Currently in development.
